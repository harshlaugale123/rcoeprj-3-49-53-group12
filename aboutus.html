ABOUT US

"Hunger is not a problem. It is an obscenity. How wonderful it is that nobody need wait a single moment before starting to improve the world." – Anne Frank


HELPING HANDS is an organisation which aims to redistribute excess food from weddings, parties, events to those who are hungry.

Helping hands works alongside Governments, Corporates with Social Responsibility on their minds, other partners and Volunteers to create a local chapter of excess food collection institution, which will be spread across various cities, addressing the two major challenges – namely urban and environmental, following the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals.
